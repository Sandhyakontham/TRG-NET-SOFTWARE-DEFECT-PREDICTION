About TRGNet

Deep dive into architecture, layer design, efficiency and storage — compared against ResNet and AlexNet.

// What is TRGNet? Novel Architecture

TRGNet (Transformer–Residual–Graph Network) is purpose-built for tabular software defect prediction. Unlike image-focused networks like ResNet and AlexNet, TRGNet applies multi-head self-attention to learn inter-metric dependencies, residual skip connections to enable stable deep training, and Graph Convolutional layers to model structural relationships between software modules.
